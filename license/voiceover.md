# Tiered Voice-Over Usage License (Commissioned Work)

*This license applies to all voice-over recordings delivered by Vorox Audio (Philipp Grunwald).  
All rights not expressly granted remain with Vorox Audio.*

**Nature of the license**  
All usage rights below are: **non-exclusive, worldwide, perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free** unless otherwise stated.

### A. Internal Use (default)

Client may use the recording internally within their organisation.  
No public or online distribution.

### B. Online Organic / Non-Commercial Use

Client may publicly distribute the recording online (website, social media, YouTube, etc.) as long as the posts are not paid advertising.

### C. Online Commercial / Paid Advertising

Client may use the recording in paid online advertising campaigns (social media ads, web ads).

### D. Full Online Buyout

Client may use the recording online, for any online purpose, without limitation except broadcast.  
Broadcast requires additional licensing.

### E. Broadcast (optional, negotiated separately)

TV, radio, cinema, or OTT broadcast requires a separate agreement.

### Modifications

Minor edits allowed.  
Substantial alteration or AI training prohibited without explicit written permission.
